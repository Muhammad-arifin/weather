About Weather App

The Weather App provides real-time weather updates for cities around the world. Powered by the OpenWeatherMap API, our app delivers accurate and up-to-date weather data including temperature, wind speed, cloud coverage, and geographical coordinates.

  • Real-time weather updates
  • Temperature details (current, minimum, and maximum)
  • Weather descriptions
  • Wind speed information
  • Cloud coverage percentage
  • Geographical coordinates of the city
